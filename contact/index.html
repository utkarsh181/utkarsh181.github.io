    Encryption for email

    You can use GPG to encrypt mail, you can get my public keys here. Save it to a file, say utkarsh.keys and run the the following command: 

    gpg --import utkarsh.keys

    Make sure to send me your public key as attachment, else I will not be able to reply to you.

    I encourage you to learn how to use encryption for your email communications and establish it as your default method for trusted contacts. You do not need to be a tech wizard to implement it. Get started with this excellent guide by the Free Software Foundation on email self-defence.
